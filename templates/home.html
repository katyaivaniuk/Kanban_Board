Let's get started 💻

A kanban board is a tool designed to help visualize work by distributing it to different stages, such as "To Do," "In Progress," and "Done." 📝


There are a lot of potential benefits of the board for project management:

  • Maximizing efficiency: 📈 Kanban boards allow you to identify pitfalls in the workflow and optimize the process of work distribution to improve efficacy.

  • Promoting teamwork:👩🏻‍💻 By providing a visual representation of the work that needs to be done, Kanban boards promote collaboration and communication among team members.

  • Improving transparency: ✨ Kanban boards provide a transparent view of the workflow and progress of tasks, allowing team members to stay informed on the status of projects.
